# Geographic Evidence Surfaces

Geographic evidence surfaces are the shared world and regional map bundles that
bring several evidence families into one visible geography.

## What These Surfaces Do

- show broad evidence patterns before a reader drops into one country bundle
- keep scope changes explicit across world, Europe-plus, and Nordic views
- carry contracts for visible layers, caveats, and filtering posture

## What They Do Not Do

These surfaces do not replace the narrower governing evidence files behind
their visible points. They are presentation surfaces with explicit contracts,
not a license to outrank sample, locality, chronology, or coordinate review.
