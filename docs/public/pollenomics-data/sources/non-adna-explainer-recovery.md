# Non-aDNA Explainer Recovery

This page exists because mixed repositories often drift toward the noisiest or
most technically difficult domain. In this project, that is usually animal
ancient DNA recovery. When that happens, the contextual source families can
remain in the tree but lose clear explanation.

## Why Recovery Was Needed

Pollen, archaeology, and boundary sources are not secondary decoration. They
are part of the public product. If their explainer pages become thin while the
animal recovery pages grow, readers get a distorted picture of what the
repository is actually for.

## What This Page Signals

- non-aDNA context remains part of the core product
- contextual source pages deserve the same reader-facing clarity as the harder recovery surfaces
- handbook breadth is a credibility issue, not just a writing preference
